Lab of AI in Biomedical Informatics

Welcome to the Lab of AI in Biomedical Informatics (LAIBI) at Yonsei University. We develop AI-driven solutions for biomedical informatics, bridging precision medicine and intelligent data analysis.

Our core research areas include:

  • Medical AI & Imaging: AI-powered diagnostics and medical image analysis for clinical applications.
  • Clinical NLP: Natural language processing applied to electronic health records and biomedical literature.
  • Precision Medicine: Data-driven approaches for personalized treatment and patient stratification.
  • Healthcare Data Science: Large-scale healthcare data analysis, predictive modeling, and federated learning.
Our work is recognized at premier conferences. We are committed to advancing biomedical AI in a collaborative and supportive environment.
